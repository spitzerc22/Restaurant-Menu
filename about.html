Origin Story

Mr. Lopez grew up in Fond Du Lac, but always loved the city and the lake front. He always had a dream to bring smiles on peoples faces with his food. He did exactly that when he opened his first restaurant in the heart of Milwaukee, right on the lake front! He is very passionate of his cooking and you can see it!
